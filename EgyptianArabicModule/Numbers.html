Core Principle: The Units-First Rule

For numbers 21-99, Arabic reads the Units digit first, then the Tens digit, connected by "wa" (و).

Units
wa (و)
Tens

Example: 74

Number Groups Breakdown

Group 2: 20-99 (Units-First)

Follows the Units-First rule with plural tens ending in -ūn.

63 ثلاثة وستون Thalāthah wa sittūn

Pronouncing Years Correctly

Reading is typically Left-to-Right: Thousands → Hundreds → (Units and Tens).

1992 ألف وتسع مئة واثنان وتسعون Alf wa tis‘u mi’ah wa ithnān wa tis‘ūn
2024 ألفان وأربعة وعشرون Alfān wa arba‘ah wa ‘ishrūn
1875 ألف وثمان مئة وخمسة وسبعون Alf wa thamān mi’ah wa khamsah wa sab‘ūn
