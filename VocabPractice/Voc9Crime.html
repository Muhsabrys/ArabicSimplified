Officer / Inspector Section

Officer
The case:
Yesterday afternoon, the teacher’s favorite book disappeared from the classroom. The book was on the teacher’s desk around 2:00. At 3:30, the book was gone. Your job is to question the suspects and find the problem in their stories.

Your job

  • Question each suspect separately.
  • Ask about time, place, and actions.
  • Write down important details.
  • Look for a time problem or contradiction.
  • At the end, explain who you suspect and why.

Suspect 2 Section

Suspect 2
You are the culprit. You took the teacher’s book. Do not say this to the officer. Your job is to sound calm and natural, but your story has a small time problem.

What really happened

You left the classroom, then secretly returned when nobody was there. You took the teacher’s favorite book from the desk. After that, you went to the club so your story would sound normal.

What you should pretend

Pretend that you went directly to the club after class. Pretend that you sat there, talked about a private topic, watched part of a program, and then returned home. Do not give too much detail unless the officer asks.
