Speaking Practice: Translation & Role-Play

Instructions: Work in pairs. Do not write down the Arabic translations. Read the English sentence, translate it in your head using the Target Word provided, and speak it aloud to your partner. Your partner will listen, process it, and reply in Arabic using their own prompts. Keep the conversation natural!
لا تقرأ الإنجليزية بصوت عالٍ. اقرأ الجملة، تذكر الكلمة المطلوبة، وتحدث بالعربية فقط!
النشاط الأول: مقابلة عمل (The Job Interview)
Scenario: A candidate is applying for a new job. The interviewer is asking about their habits, expectations, and work style. The candidate is a bit demanding.

Role A: المدير (The Interviewer)

Role B: المُرشَّح للوظيفة (The Candidate)

دور المدير (Interviewer's Prompts)

English Sentence (Translate & Speak in Arabic) Target Words (Use these!)
1. Why did you come late to the interview today? جاء / متأخر / مقابلة
2. Do you understand the work here? يفهم
3. When do you usually wake up? Do you stay up late? عادةً / يُصبح / يسهر
4. What salary do you expect if you work quickly? مُرتَّب / إذا / بسرعة
5. Our time is important. Can you keep the office organized? وقت / مُرتَّب

دور المُرشَّح (Candidate's Prompts)

English Sentence (Translate & Speak in Arabic) Target Words (Use these!)
1. Frankly, I haven't worked since a year ago. بصراحة / مُنذُ
2. I need a lot of money, to live like a king. مَلِك
3. I do not like to stay up late, I prefer sleep. السهر / النوم
4. Is it possible to have a break for prayer? يُمكن أن / استراحة / صلاة
5. If the work is boring, I want more money! مُمِل / أكثر
النشاط الثاني: تخطيط اللقاء (Weekend Plans)
Scenario: Two friends are trying to figure out what to do tonight. Friend A is very energetic, but Friend B is tired and making excuses.

Role A: الصديق النشيط (Energetic Friend)

Role B: الصديق المتعب (Tired/Busy Friend)

دور الصديق الأول (Friend A's Prompts)

English Sentence (Translate & Speak in Arabic) Target Words (Use these!)
1. Is it possible that we meet tonight? يُمكن أن / يُقابل / الليلة
2. We haven't enjoyed our time since last month! استمتع / مُنذُ
3. Do you want to watch the football match? مُباراة
4. We can pray at the big mosque, then go. جامع / صلاة
5. Come on! We have a lot of time! وقت

دور الصديق الثاني (Friend B's Prompts)

English Sentence (Translate & Speak in Arabic) Target Words (Use these!)
1. Frankly, the matches are very boring. بصراحة / مباريات / مُمِل
2. If we go, I will come late. إذا / جاء / متأخر
3. I have appointments. I must finish quickly. مواعيد / بسرعة
4. I need to sleep, I do not want to stay up late nights. ينام / يسهر / ليالي
5. I prefer reading more than going out. أكثر
