Understanding the Root-and-Pattern System

Most Arabic verbs are derived from a 3-letter root (represented by ف - ع - ل). By morphing this root into one of the ten primary Awzan (patterns), speakers convey precise semantic shifts like causation, repetition, intensity, or reciprocity.
