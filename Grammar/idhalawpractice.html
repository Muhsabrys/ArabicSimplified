Conditional Sentences Activity

إذا ولو

A collaborative problem-solving task for intermediate learners. Plan a class trip, navigate hypothetical problems, and present your group's final decision.

Activity Instructions

Read these steps with your group before beginning the worksheet.

Group Size 3–4 students per group
Objective Agree on one class trip
Grammar Focus إذا (Real) vs. لو (Unreal)
Language Rule Speak entirely in Arabic!
Your Mission: The university has given your class a budget and time for a field trip. Your group must review the options, discuss what will realistically happen (إذا), imagine what you would do if things went wrong (لو), and present a final choice to the class.
1

Grammar Review: The Rules of Conditionals

Review the difference between the two main conditional words before you start planning.

إذا (Real / Possible)

Use إذا for a condition that can actually happen. It is exactly like saying "if" or "when" in a realistic scenario.

إذا + فعل ماضٍ، فسوف / سـ + فعل مضارع
إذا كان الجو جميلًا، سنذهب إلى الحديقة.
(If the weather is nice, we will go to the park.)

لو (Hypothetical / Unreal)

Use لو for an imaginary, impossible, or highly unlikely condition. It is like saying "If I were..." or "If we had..."

لو + فعل ماضٍ، لـ + فعل ماضٍ / لَـ + مضارع
لو كان عندي وقت أكثر، لسافرتُ إلى مصر.
(If I had more time, I would have traveled to Egypt.)

Step 5: Solve the Problems

Read each problem. How would you handle it realistically (إذا)? What would you do in a perfect, hypothetical world (لو)? Write two solutions per problem.

Example / مثال

المشكلة: الوقت قصير جدًا. (The time is very short.)

Real Plan (إذا): إذا كان الوقت قصيرًا، سنشاهد فيلمًا في الجامعة.
(If the time is short, we will watch a movie on campus.)

Dream Plan (لو): لو كان عندنا وقت طويل، لذهبنا إلى الحديقة ثم المطعم.
(If we had a long time, we would go to the park then the restaurant.)

المشكلة: الجو قد يكون ممطرًا يوم الرحلة.
(Problem: The weather might be rainy.)
المشكلة: بعض الطلاب لا يريدون دفع أكثر من ١٠ دولارات.
(Problem: Some students don't want to pay more than $10.)
المشكلة: الطلاب يريدون ممارسة الكلام بالعربية، وليس الاستماع فقط.
(Problem: Students want to practice speaking, not just listening.)
المشكلة: بعض الطلاب لا يحبون المشي مسافات طويلة.
(Problem: Some students don't like walking long distances.)
