Grammar Mechanics Review

How to form and position the comparative pattern within real-world contexts.

The General Template

أَفْعَل

Regular three-letter adjectives correspond directly to this paradigm. It remains masculine singular during direct comparative tasks, regardless of gender.

كَبِير (Big) ← أَكْبَر
صَغِير (Small) ← أَصْغَر
قَرِيب (Near) ← أَقْرَب

Comparative Structure

X أَفْعَل مِن Y

Place the preposition مِن (than/from) directly after the adjective form to construct comparison assertions.

القاهرةُ أَكْبَرُ مِن بلومنجتون.
Cairo is bigger than Bloomington.
مريم أَكْبَرُ مِن ليلى.
Maryam is older than Laila.

Superlative Structure

أَفْعَل + اسم مجرور

Prefix the أَفْعَل form directly to an indefinite genitive noun to form superlative statements ("the cheapest X").

أَفْضَلُ طالِبٍ
The best student
أَقَلُّ مَبْلَغٍ
The least amount (of money)
