الإضافة: معرفة ونكرة

Definite and Indefinite Idafa
Interactive Tool: The rule states that the definiteness of an Idafa depends solely on its final word.

In the story in this lesson, you heard descriptions of houses containing many إضافات, such as:

غرفة المعيشة
غرفة الاستقبال
غرفة النوم

As you have come to expect, these إضافات are all definite. In addition to the definite إضافات, however, you also heard a couple of إضافات that are not definite: حمّام سباحة (literally, "a pool of swimming"), and ٤ غرف نوم. We know that these phrases are indefinite because the last noun in each إضافة is indefinite, having neither ال nor a possessive pronoun. (Even though both of these nouns are مصدر, this is one case in which المصدر may be indefinite.) The indefiniteness of the final noun makes the entire إضافة indefinite. We can make all the definite إضافات above indefinite just by removing ال from the final noun:

غرفة معيشة a family room
غرفة استقبال a formal living room
غرفة نوم a bedroom

What contexts call for an indefinite إضافة? Think about when we use indefinite nouns in English. When would you say "a bedroom" instead of "the bedroom?" In Arabic, two of the most frequently occurring contexts in which indefinite nouns and إضافات are used include existential sentences (e.g., "there is" or "there isn't") and counting with numbers from ٣ to ١٠, including the use of عدّة several:

عندي غرفة نوم كبيرة.
وراء المنزل حمّام سباحة.
فيه ٤ غرف نوم.
هناك عدّة حلول ممكنة.

To express meaning accurately, it is important to think about the choice of definite and indefinite nouns and noun phrases. For example, do you mean to say a lunch break or the lunch break? Your listener will interpret these two phrases differently. Sometimes the choice between definite and indefinite إضافة carries important implications. You might call someone who does something nice for you ابن حلال or بنت حلال but not ابن الحلال or بنت الحلال. The definite ابن الحلال is the person you should or could marry, whereas an indefinite ابن حلال is one of a number of possible candidates:

ابن/بنت حلال a possible mate, i.e., a great person
ابن/بنت الحلال the one for you
Key Rule: Grammatically, the definiteness or indefiniteness of الإضافة depends solely on its final word. When that final word is definite, either with ال or with a pronoun, الإضافة is definite. When the final word is indefinite, الإضافة is indefinite.

Indefinite إضافات behave in sentences just like indefinite nouns. For example, adjectives modifying them are indefinite and agree with the noun they modify. In the following sentences, the adjectives كبيرة and جميلة are مؤنث because they modify غرفة:

أريد غرفة نوم كبيرة.
هذه غرفة استقبال جميلة.
Remember to pronounce ة on all nonfinal words of any إضافة.

Finally, notice that أفعل التفضيل is also an indefinite إضافة in the superlative construction. Although the superlative is definite in English, it is indefinite in Arabic and behaves accordingly:

سنستأجر (رح نستأجر ، حنأجّر) أحسن شقة بالشام كلها. أين توجد أقدم كنيسة كاثوليكية في العالم العربي؟ أختي أخذت (أخَدِت، خَدِت) أكبر غرفة نوم في البيت.
