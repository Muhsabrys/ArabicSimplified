The Ten Verb Forms & Their Verbal Nouns

Arabic derives verbs from three-letter roots through a system of ten principal patterns (أوزان). Each form carries a predictable grammatical meaning — causative, reflexive, reciprocal, intensive, and more — allowing fluent manipulation of meaning from a single root.
