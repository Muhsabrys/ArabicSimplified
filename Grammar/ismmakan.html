اسم المكان

The Noun of Place

You have learned a number of words that share وَزن مَفْعَل(ة), including:

مَكْتَب مَكْتَبَة مَدْرَسَة مَطْبَخ مَسْبَح مَرْكَز مَسْرَح

What do the meanings of these words have in common? They all refer to places: Places where we write, read books, study, and so forth.

You can see that two of the nouns above end in ة. This addition is not common. Sometimes it permits a second meaning to be derived from a root, such as the case of the word مَكْتَبَة, which has a different meaning than مَكْتَب. In other cases, such as مَدْرَسَة, there is no corresponding اسم مكان without ة.

Since the use of ة is uncommon, we will focus on forming اسم المكان without it, and will specify ة in the few cases in which it occurs.

The Core Idea

What is a Noun of Place?

اسم المكان

A noun of place is a noun derived from a verb. It names the place where the action of that verb happens.

Verb
كَتَبَ
to write
action + place
Noun of Place
مَكْتَب
office; a place for writing

Arabic also has a noun of time, اسم الزمان, which names the time when an action happens. Nouns of place and time are formed by the same rules. Context tells us whether the word refers to a place or a time.

مَوْعِدُ الدَّرْسِ
the time of the lesson
مَوْضِعُ الكِتابِ
the place or position of the book

Regular Formation

Forming the Noun of Place from a Three-Letter Verb

For most three-letter verbs, look at the vowel in the middle of the present-tense verb. This determines whether the noun follows مَفْعِل or مَفْعَل.

Rule 1: Use مَفْعِل with يَفْعِل

يَفْعِل ← مَفْعِل

When the middle vowel of the present-tense stem is a kasra, the noun of place normally follows the pattern مَفْعِل.

Past Verb Present Verb Noun of Place Meaning
جَلَسَيَجْلِسُمَجْلِسsitting room; council
نَزَلَيَنْزِلُمَنْزِلhome; place where one stays
عَرَضَيَعْرِضُمَعْرِضexhibition hall; gallery
هَبَطَيَهْبِطُمَهْبِطlanding place
رَجَعَيَرْجِعُمَرْجِعplace of return; reference

Rule 2: Use مَفْعَل with يَفْعَل or يَفْعُل

يَفْعَل / يَفْعُل ← مَفْعَل

When the middle vowel of the present-tense stem is a fatḥa or a ḍamma, the noun of place normally follows the pattern مَفْعَل.

Past Verb Present Verb Noun of Place Meaning
كَتَبَيَكْتُبُمَكْتَبoffice; desk
دَخَلَيَدْخُلُمَدْخَلentrance
خَرَجَيَخْرُجُمَخْرَجexit
لَعِبَيَلْعَبُمَلْعَبplaying field; stadium
شَرِبَيَشْرَبُمَشْرَبdrinking place
ذَهَبَيَذْهَبُمَذْهَبway; school of thought
طَبَخَيَطْبُخُمَطْبَخkitchen
سَبَحَيَسْبَحُمَسْبَحswimming pool

Important: Do not choose the pattern by looking only at the past-tense verb. Check the present-tense form: يَجْلِسُ gives مَجْلِس, but يَكْتُبُ gives مَكْتَب.

Weak Roots

What Happens with Weak Verbs?

1. Verbs beginning with و

When a three-letter verb begins with و and ends with a regular consonant, the noun of place or time usually follows مَفْعِل.

وَعَدَ – يَعِدُ ← مَوْعِد
appointment; appointed time
وَضَعَ – يَضَعُ ← مَوْضِع
place; position
وَقَعَ – يَقَعُ ← مَوْقِع
location; site

2. Verbs ending in a weak letter

When the final root letter is weak, the noun usually follows a form related to مَفْعَل, often ending in ى.

أَوَى – يَأْوِي ← مَأْوَى
shelter; refuge
رَمَى – يَرْمِي ← مَرْمَى
goal; target; throwing place
سَعَى – يَسْعَى ← مَسْعَى
place or course of effort
جَرَى – يَجْرِي ← مَجْرَى
course; channel; place where something flows
ثَوَى – يَثْوِي ← مَثْوَى
dwelling place; resting place

Common Irregular Forms

Some Words Do Not Follow the Expected Vowel

Some frequently used nouns of place have a kasra or fatḥa that is different from what the regular rule predicts. These forms are established by usage, so it is best to learn them as vocabulary.

مَسْجِد
mosque
مَسْكِن
dwelling; residence
مَشْرِق
east; place of sunrise
مَغْرِب
west; sunset time
مَطْلِع
rising place or time
مَرْفِق
facility; public service place
مَنْبِت
place where something grows
مَنْسِك
place or rite of worship

For example, the fully regular prediction for سَجَدَ – يَسْجُدُ would be مَسْجَد, but the common word for “mosque” is مَسْجِد.

Derived Verb Forms

Nouns of Place from Verbs Longer than Three Letters

With verbs longer than three root-pattern letters, the noun of place normally has the same form as the passive participle. Begin with the present-tense verb, replace the present-tense prefix with مُـ, and place a fatḥa on the letter before the final letter.

Present Verb
يَجْتَمِعُ
→
Noun of Place
مُجْتَمَع
Verb Present Noun of Place Meaning
اِجْتَمَعَيَجْتَمِعُمُجْتَمَعgathering place; community
اِنْطَلَقَيَنْطَلِقُمُنْطَلَقstarting point
اِسْتَقَرَّيَسْتَقِرُّمُسْتَقَرّsettlement; resting place
اِسْتَوْدَعَيَسْتَوْدِعُمُسْتَوْدَعwarehouse; storage place
اِسْتَشْفَىيَسْتَشْفِيمُسْتَشْفَىhospital; place where one seeks healing
اِلْتَقَىيَلْتَقِيمُلْتَقَىmeeting place; forum

Context Determines the Meaning

The same form may refer to a place, a time, a passive participle, or another established noun. Read the entire sentence rather than translating the form mechanically.

هٰذَا مَوْعِدُ الطَّائِرَةِ.
This is the time of the flight.
هٰذَا مُلْتَقَى الطُّلَّابِ.
This is the students’ meeting place.
البَابُ مُغْلَقٌ.
The door is closed. Here the form is a passive participle, not a place.

Common Plural Patterns

Many nouns of place on مَفْعَل or مَفْعِل have a broken plural on مَفاعِل. However, not every noun uses this plural, so learn the plural with the singular.

Quick Reference

How Do I Choose the Form?

1

Is the verb a regular three-letter verb? Check its present-tense middle vowel.

2

يَفْعِل usually gives مَفْعِل.

3

يَفْعَل or يَفْعُل usually gives مَفْعَل.

4

For longer verbs, use the passive-participle pattern beginning with مُـ.

Finally, check whether the word is a common irregular form. High-frequency words such as مَسْجِد and مَسْكِن should be memorized.

Useful Nouns of Place

Additional examples for everyday communication.

The most useful rule for learners is to connect the noun of place to the present-tense form of the verb. Some words have become independent vocabulary items, so their modern meanings may be broader than the literal meaning of the original verb.
