Arabic Conditionals: First-Person Focus

A personalized guide featuring present-tense routines (إذا) and counterfactual imaginations (لو)

إذا

Present Habitual Habits

When using إذا to state a factual condition or real-life habit, both the condition and result can stay in the present tense.

💡 Grammar Check: Notice how a plain, unassisted present tense verb in the result requires no prefix (no فـ). It maps out an immediate, automatic cause-and-effect relationship.

لو

First-Person Imaginations

When daydreaming or stating counterfactuals with لو ("If I were... / If I had..."), verbs standardly utilize past-tense framing to indicate unreality.

⚠️ Grammar Check: Affirmative past results receive the prefix لَـ (La-) with first-person conjugation tokens (-تُ), while negated past results drop it for لَمَا (La-ma).

■ Real Present Tense Examples (Habits & Routines)

إذا عِنْدِي وَاجِبٌ، أُغْلِقُ هَاتِفِي.

Idha 'indi wajib, ughliqu hatifi.

If I have homework, I close my phone.

إذا أَشْعُرُ بِالتَّعَبِ، أَشْرَبُ قَهْوَةً.

Idha ash'uru bi-ta'abi, ashrabu qahwah.

If I feel tired, I drink coffee.

إذا يَزُورُنِي صَدِيقِي، أَطْبُخُ العَشَاءَ.

Idha yazuruni sadiqi, atbukhu al-'asha'.

If my friend visits me, I cook dinner.

إذا أَجِدُ وَقْتَ فَرَاغٍ، أَقْرَأُ كِتَاباً.

Idha ajidu waqta faragh, aqra'u kitaban.

If I find free time, I read a book.

■ Hypothetical & Counterfactual Examples (لو)

Unlike factual present situations, لو takes you into an alternate reality or past regret. Even if you are imagining a current situation ("If I were rich right now..."), Arabic uses the past tense verb structure directly following لو to establish that it is an unreal hypothesis.

لَوْ كُنْتُ غَنِيّاً، لَـسَافَرْتُ حَوْلَ العَالَمِ.

Law kuntu ghaniyyan, la-safartu hawla al-'alam.

If I were rich, I would travel around the world.

Context: Imagining an alternate reality right now.

لَوْ دَرَسْتُ بِجِدٍّ، لَـنَجَحْتُ فِي الِامْتِحَانِ.

Law darastu bi-jiddin, la-najahtu fi al-imtihan.

If I had studied hard, I would have passed the exam.

Context: Expressing a past regret about something that didn't happen.

لَوْ عَرَفْتُ الحَقِيقَةَ، لَـمَا تَكَلَّمْتُ مَعَهُ.

Law 'araftu al-haqiqah, la-ma takallamtu ma'ahu.

If I had known the truth, I would not have spoken with him.

Context: Negative counterfactual. Notice the use of لَمَا.

لَوْ كَانَ عِنْدِي وَقْتٌ البَارِحَةَ، لَـزُرْتُ وَالِدَتِي.

Law kana 'indi waqtun al-barihah, la-zurtu walidati.

If I had had time yesterday, I would have visited my mother.

Context: A missed opportunity in the past.

■ Workbook Conversions (First-Person Perspectives)

All sentences are reframed from the perspective of "I / Me", showcasing present-tense routines, future projections, and hypothetical alternative timelines.

Prompt 1: My Classes Canceled
إذا + Future Projection (Requires فـ)

إذا أُلْغِيَتْ دُرُوسِي غَداً بِسَبَبِ الطَّقْسِ فَـسَأَبْقَى فِي Mَنْزِلِي.

If my classes are canceled tomorrow due to the weather, [then] I will stay home.

لو + Hypothetical Past Counterfactual (Takes لَـ)

لَوْ أُلْغِيَتْ دُرُوسِي لَـنِمْتُ طَوِيلاً.

If my classes had been canceled, I would have slept for a long time.

Prompt 2: Receiving a $500 Check
إذا + Future Plan (Requires فـ)

إذا حَصَلْتُ عَلى شِيكٍ بِـ 500 دُولارٍ فَـسَوْفَ أَشْتَرِي كُتُباً جَدِيدَةً.

If I receive a check for $500, [then] I will buy new books.

لو + Hypothetical Imagination (Takes لَـ)

لَوْ حَصَلْتُ عَلى الشِّيكِ المَاضِي لَـدَفَعْتُ إِيجَارَ شَقَّتِي.

If I had received the check back then, I would have paid my apartment rent.

Prompt 3: Stranger Friend Request
إذا + Definite Future Rejection (Requires فـ)

إذا أَرَادَ شَخْصٌ لا أَعْرِفُهُ صَدَاقَتِي عَلى فِيسْبُوك فَـلَنْ أَقْبَلَ الطَّلَبَ.

If someone I don't know wants to become my friend on Facebook, [then] I will not accept the request.

لو + Historical Counterfactual (Takes لَمَا)

لَوْ أَرْسَلَ ذَلِكَ الغَرِيبُ طَلَبَ صَدَاقَةٍ لَـمَا تَجَاوَبْتُ مَعَهُ.

If that stranger had sent a friend request, I would not have interacted with him.

Prompt 4: Tired Before An Exam
إذا + Present Habitual Choice (NO فـ)

إذا كُنْتُ مُتْعَبَةً جِدّاً وَلَدَيَّ امْتِحَانٌ غَداً أَدْرُسُ بِجِدٍّ رَغْمَ التَّعَبِ.

Whenever I am very tired and have an exam tomorrow, I study hard despite the exhaustion.

لو + Hypothetical Alternative (Takes لَـ)

لَوْ نِمْتُ مُبَكِّراً البَارِحَةَ لَـنَجَحْتُ بِامْتِيَازٍ اليَوْمَ.

If I had slept early last night, I would have passed with excellence today.

Prompt 5: Staying Up Late For A Friend
إذا + Present Tense Action (NO فـ)

إذا طَلَبَ صَدِيقِي المُسَاعَدَةَ أَسْهَرُ مَعَهُ طَوَالَ اللَّيْلِ.

If my friend asks for help, I stay up with him all night long.

لو + Unfulfilled Wish Concept (Takes لَـ)

لَوْ كَانَتْ عِنْدِي فُرْصَةٌ لَـسَاعَدْتُهُ فِي مَشَاكِلِهِ.

If I had the chance, I would have helped him with his problems.

Prompt 6: Meeting Someone Special
إذا + Real Present Feeling (NO فـ)

إذا أُقَابِلُ شَخْصاً مُمَيَّزاً أَشْعُرُ بِالسَّعَادَةِ الغَامِرَةِ.

When I meet someone special, I feel overwhelming happiness.

لو + Imaginary Romance Dynamic (Takes لَـ)

لَوْ قَابَلْتُ شَخْصاً مُمَيَّزاً فِي المَاضِي لَـتَزَوَّجْتُهُ فَوْراً.

If I had met someone special in the past, I would have married them immediately.

Prompt 7: Busy Best Friend
إذا + Future Coping Layout (Requires فـ)

إذا انْشَغَلَ صَدِيقِي المُفَضَّلُ عَنِّي غَداً فَـسَأَعْمَلُ بِمُفْرَدِي.

If my best friend is too busy for me tomorrow, [then] I will work by myself.

لو + Gracious Understanding Check (Takes لَمَا)

لَوْ انْشَغَلَ صَدِيقِي عَنِّي بِسَبَبِ ظُرُوفِهِ ...لَـمَا غَضِبْتُ مِنْهُ أَبَداً.

If my friend had been too busy for me due to his circumstances, I would never have been angry with him.

Keep up the good work! Notice how switching to first-person singular shifts the focus directly to your personal routines and reflections.
